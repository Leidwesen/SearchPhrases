This is a collection of all Pokemon GO search terms / search phrases / search strings, their uses and translations, their bugs, and related links.

There's no shortages of lists of these phrases on the internet, but all of them are lacking. Even Niantic's official list and the best alternative I could find (Bulbapedia) are missing phrases and functionalities.

This site aims to be the definitive list of all things Pokemon GO search related.

Translations:

For translations of most phrases, change the language on the official Niantic List.
For translations of all phrases, consult this spreadsheet.

For translations of most moves in most languages, consult this Bulbapedia entry.
For translations of all moves in all languages, consult this spreadsheet.

For translation of all pokemon names in most languages, consult this Pokewiki entry.
For translation of most pokemon names in all languages, consult this spreadsheet.

Complexity:

For more complex searches involving parentheses, they can be converted into Pokemon GO's syntax using u/Lebeg134's converter

IVs:

The IV filters introduced in July 2022 can only search on ranges, so tools for finer IV searches can still be useful. They use CP+HP combinations to do so.

Tools such as ark42, rplus, and gohub help with that, though they are becoming outdated. u/Trummler12 maintains spreadsheets of IV search strings

For PvP IV search strings, tools such as PvPIV's are useful, and u/Errubki maintains spreadsheets of PVP IV search strings.

Other:

For converting from species names to numbers, Pogostring is useful.

For that plus maintaining more complex trade strings (filtering by shiny, gender, and regional form), see Fly33's Tool.
